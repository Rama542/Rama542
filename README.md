## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a cloud-native developer with a deep focus on **observability** and **distributed systems telemetry**.
I contribute to the CNCF ecosystem because the problems are genuinely fascinating — not to pad a resume.

My work sits at the intersection of **distributed tracing**, **metrics pipelines**, and **cloud-native infrastructure** —
the foundations that make modern systems understandable at scale.

- 🔭 **Active contributor** to **OpenTelemetry** under CNCF
- 📡 Working on **receivers, exporters, and tracing pipelines** in the OTel Collector
- 🎯 **Primary LFX target → OpenTelemetry** (Summer 2026)
- ☸️ Part of the broader CNCF contributor community
- 📚 Currently deep-diving into **eBPF-based auto-instrumentation** and **OTel Protocol (OTLP)**

## 🌟 Primary Goal — OpenTelemetry

OpenTelemetry is solving one of the most important problems in cloud-native:
**vendor-neutral, unified observability** across traces, metrics, and logs.

I've studied the Collector architecture, understand the pipeline model
(receivers → processors → exporters), and I'm actively contributing to real issues.

> 🎯 **Goal:** Contribute to the OTel Collector, SDKs, and auto-instrumentation pipelines.
